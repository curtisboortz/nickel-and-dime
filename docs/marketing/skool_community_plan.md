# Skool Community Plan: The Macro Investor's Desk

## Community Setup
- **Name:** The Macro Investor's Desk
- **Tier:** Free (upgrade to $99/month if you exceed limits)
- **URL:** skool.com/macro-investors-desk (or similar)
- **Header Image:** Dashboard screenshot with macro pulse cards
- **Description:** A free community for self-directed investors who follow the macro picture. Weekly briefings, portfolio reviews, and market analysis. Powered by Nickel & Dime.

## Pinned Introduction Post

**Title:** Welcome to The Macro Investor's Desk

**Body:**
Hey everyone, I'm Curtis. I'm building Nickel & Dime, a portfolio tracker designed for investors who follow the macro picture.

This community is where we discuss the macro side of investing: yield curves, gold/silver ratios, Fed policy, inflation data, sentiment indicators, and how they all connect to portfolio positioning.

**What you'll get here:**
- Weekly macro briefing (every Monday) with data from FRED, sentiment gauges, and market observations
- Monthly portfolio review thread where members share their allocation and get feedback
- Discussion on economic events as they happen
- Early access to new features and blog posts

**The tool is optional.** You can get value from this community whether you use Nickel & Dime or not. But if you're curious: nickelanddime.io (free tier available).

Rules: Be respectful. No financial advice, just sharing data and perspectives. No pump-and-dump or crypto shilling.

## Weekly Content Schedule

### Monday: Macro Briefing
Post a summary of the week's key data:
- S&P 500, gold, silver, BTC week-over-week changes
- Yield curve status (inverted/flat/normal, 2s10s spread)
- Fear & Greed reading
- Key economic events this week (from the calendar)
- Gold/silver ratio update
Include screenshots from Nickel & Dime dashboard.

### Wednesday: Deep Dive
Pick one topic per week:
- "What the yield curve is telling us right now"
- "Gold/silver ratio: historical context"
- "CAPE ratio vs. S&P 500 returns"
- "Reading the Sahm Rule"
- "How to think about dollar strength (DXY) for your portfolio"

### Friday: Open Discussion
- "What are you watching this weekend?"
- "Share your allocation (optional)"
- "Questions thread: ask anything macro"

### Monthly: Portfolio Review (1st of each month)
- Members share their allocation (categories only, no specific amounts required)
- Community provides feedback
- Compare against popular templates (All-Weather, 60/40, Permanent)

## Growth Strategy
1. **Weeks 1-2:** Seed with content. Post daily, even with no members.
2. **Weeks 3-4:** Share in relevant Reddit threads (when contextually appropriate). Add link to Twitter bio.
3. **Month 2:** Cross-promote with blog posts. Each blog post mentions the Skool community.
4. **Month 3:** Consider inviting 2-3 FinTwit creators as co-moderators to bring their audience.
5. **Ongoing:** Every Nickel & Dime user gets an invite link in their welcome email.

## Metrics
- Month 1: 25-50 members
- Month 3: 100-200 members
- Month 6: 500+ members
- Engagement: 10+ comments per weekly briefing
